Local activities


Topgolf
Topgolf is a global sports entertainment company headquartered in Dallas, Texas. The facility primarily offers a game that can be played by anyone regardless of their golfing ability. Players hit micro-chipped golf balls similar to a driving range, however they aim for targets and score points based on the game type selected. Servers offer a full selection of food and drinks while playing in climate controlled bays. The facility also offers a full sports lounge, apparel store, and virtual game simulators to enhance your experience.

Winery Tours
More than 13 wineries in Leesburg and dozens more in the vicinity. Several transportation companies offer wine tours, ranging from commercial passenger vans to limousines to suit your needs.

Historic Downtown Leesburg
Known for being one of the best preserved and most picturesque downtowns in Virginia, Downtown Leesburg is your everyday destination: historic sites and museums, vibrant culinary scene, large selection of breweries, award-winning wineries, boutiques, antiques and range of startup to enterprise size companies.
